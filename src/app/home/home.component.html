Personal Finance Management APP by Uroš Zukančić
Angular Angular Materials

Personal Finance Management is a set of activities performed by the customer on its financial data for the purpose of getting clear picture of the incomes/expenses trends, as well as enabling customer to manage monthly budget and personal financial goals.

Personal Finance Management starts with categorization of transactions into income, expense and savings & investment categories and subcategories. Majority of transactions should be automatically categorized, allowing the customer to manage categorization manually and using user categorization rules. Each transaction has a category and optionally a subcategory assigned.
